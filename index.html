Paper Review

Most of papers listed below were part of the Ph.D. class "Empirical Research in Accounting" taught by the esteemed Prof. John Mcinnis. Prof. McInnis graciously provided me with two syllabi from his class, and I read the papers organized by topic. I grouped the papers based on their themes and added tags to highlight the most memorable points for each. Additionally, I have included the pros and cons for each paper as well as slides I made.

Some papers listed below were from Ph.D. class "BEHAVIORAL RESEARCH IN ACCOUNTING" taught by the esteemed Prof. Brian White. Since I would not do behavioral Research, I read those for trying to using machine learning, deep learning & advanced computation tools to do behavioral questions in empirical ways. Still there are sildes, tags. cons and pros for thos papers

Other paper could be works of Bryan Kelly or some works related to alternative data, machine learning and other methods in accounting research. I consider this part of papers my toolkit.
