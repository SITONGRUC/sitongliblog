Two types of research related to machine learning in finance
May 2024

In this post, I will briefly explore two types of research related to machine learning in finance. The research can be categorized into two main areas: firstly, studies that integrate machine learning with financial theories; and secondly, research that employs machine learning primarily for data processing or regression analysis. Finally, I will provide a list of the papers I have reviewed on this topic.
